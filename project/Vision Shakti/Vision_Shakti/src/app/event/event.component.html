Our Events

Events

Every year, Vision Shakti hosts a number of events to raise awareness and donations to fundmicroloans, training and services that enables women to learn the new skills as well as they can start businesses, support their families and strengthen their communities.

Breaking Barriers

Breaking Barriers: Women's Empowerment Symposium" fosters dialogue and action for gender equality. Empowering women through education, leadership, and advocacy, it aims to dismantle obstacles hindering progress towards inclusivity and empowerment..

Leading with Grace

Women's Empowerment Summit" champions women's leadership, fostering grace and resilience. Through inspiration and empowerment, it equips women with tools to excel in diverse spheres, driving societal change.

Women in Focus

Igniting Change, Inspiring Growth" spotlights female leadership, catalyzing transformative change and fostering personal and professional development. Through inspiration and empowerment, it fuels a brighter, more inclusive future.

SheLeads

Empowering Women for Tomorrow" inspires female leadership, equipping women with skills and confidence for future success. Through mentorship and opportunities, it nurtures a generation of empowered women ready to lead.

Rise & Shine

Empowering Women Through Leadership" ignites female empowerment by cultivating leadership skills and fostering confidence. Through mentorship and guidance, it empowers women to thrive and lead with resilience and grace..

The Power Paradigm

Women Empowering Women" celebrates female solidarity, fostering a culture of support and collaboration. Through mentorship and mutual empowerment, it catalyzes transformative change, amplifying women's voices and influence.
