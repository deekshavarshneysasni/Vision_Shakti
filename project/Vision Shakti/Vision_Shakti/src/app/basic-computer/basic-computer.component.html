Basic Computer

Introducing basic computer skills in rural areas can enhance educational opportunities by providing access to online learning resources, educational software, and digital libraries. It can also facilitate distance learning programs, allowing students to access quality education remotely.

Basic computer skills are increasingly essential for various jobs across different sectors. By providing training in rural areas, individuals can acquire skills relevant to modern workplaces, improving their employability and income-earning potential.

Computers and the internet can bridge the information gap between rural and urban areas. With basic computer skills, rural residents can access a wide range of information on topics such as agriculture techniques, healthcare, government services, and market prices, empowering them to make informed decisions and improve their livelihoods.

Impact 2022-2023


Basic computer usage provides access to a wealth of information available on the internet. Rural residents can learn about agricultural practices, healthcare information, educational resources, and market trends, empowering them to make informed decisions and improve their lives.

Computers in rural areas can facilitate distance learning programs, online courses, and access to digital educational materials. This expands educational opportunities for students who may not have access to traditional educational institutions, allowing them to acquire new skills and knowledge.

Basic computer skills enable rural residents to communicate more effectively with individuals and organizations beyond their immediate communities. This can lead to improved networking, collaboration, and access to support networks for various needs, including healthcare, education, and employment.

Overall, the impact of using basic computers in rural areas is multifaceted, contributing to socio-economic development, empowerment, and improved quality of life for rural residents.
